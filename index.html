Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us when creating an account, including your username, email address, and password. Chat messages you send are processed through our AI backend but are stored locally in your browser.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to provide and maintain the Velora service, authenticate your identity, and improve your experience. We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

Account information is stored securely via Supabase. Chat history is stored in your browser's localStorage and is never uploaded to our servers.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Supabase — authentication and user data
  • OpenRouter — AI model inference (messages are processed but not stored)
  • Vercel — hosting and edge functions

5. Cookies

We use session cookies for authentication purposes only. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact the creator via the About page.
